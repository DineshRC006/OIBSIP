How it works

Three steps, one shared record

The same process a villager, an official, and an administrator all watch unfold together — nothing routed into a private inbox.

Step 1

Raise it

A villager describes the issue — a broken hand pump, an unpaved road, a delayed ration card — and it's logged instantly with a ticket number.

Step 2

Route it

The right department official is notified automatically, based on category and location — no visiting three offices to find who's responsible.

Step 3

Track it

Status updates — received, in progress, resolved — are visible to the villager and to the admin dashboard, in the open.

Built for three roles

One platform, every side of the counter

Villagers, officials, and administrators each get a dashboard suited to what they need to do — not one generic form for everyone.

⚑

For Villagers

File complaints in your own words, check status anytime, and see announcements from your local panchayat — all from one place.

⚖

For Officials

See every complaint assigned to your department, update its status, and clear your queue without paper registers or phone calls.

📊

For Administrators

View resolution times across departments, spot recurring issues by village, and publish official announcements to every resident.

🔔

Status you can trust

Every complaint carries a visible timeline. Nothing is marked resolved without the villager who filed it being able to confirm it.
